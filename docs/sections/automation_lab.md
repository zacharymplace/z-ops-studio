# Automation Lab  
### High-leverage automations built for Finance, Systems, and Operations.

The Automation Lab is a collection of practical, lightweight workflow automations designed to eliminate manual work, reduce errors, and accelerate recurring processes.

These demos highlight what Z$ Ops Studio builds for real teams—clean, reliable, and maintainable systems that create meaningful time savings.

---

## 1. Daily Cash Position Automation  
**Focus:** Treasury, visibility, morning readiness  
A lightweight script that pulls bank balances, calculates variances, formats a standardized blurb, and prepares it for reporting or email distribution.  
**Outcome:** Reduces daily cash prep from 45 minutes to <5 minutes.  
**→ View Demo**

---

## 2. Reconciliation Assistant  
**Focus:** Month-end close, data validation, audit support  
A cross-sheet comparison engine that identifies mismatches, missing records, and exceptions—then outputs a clean “Exceptions” tab for review.  
**Outcome:** Removes hours of manual scanning and reduces reconciliation risk.  
**→ View Demo**

---

## 3. Vendor Run-Rate Automation Engine  
**Focus:** Forecasting, AP spend modeling, FP&A  
Automatically calculates vendor averages, applies inflation and volume drivers, and generates a forecast table.  
**Outcome:** Clean, repeatable driver-based vendor forecasting without human error.  
**→ View Demo**

---

## 4. Adaptive → Sheets Sync Utility  
**Focus:** FP&A reporting, version control, systems integration  
Reads Adaptive export files, normalizes headers, cleans data, and loads it into a structured sheet for downstream reporting.  
**Outcome:** Eliminates manual exports and produces a single source of truth.  
**→ View Demo**

---

## 5. Close Task Tracker & SLA Monitor  
**Focus:** Month-end operations, visibility, team readiness  
Scans close tasks, applies SLA logic, flags at-risk or overdue items, and supports automated email reminders.  
**Outcome:** Real-time visibility and smoother close cycles.
