This project focused on designing and executing the organization-wide implementation of Stable ‚Äî a digital mailroom platform used to centralize, digitize, classify, and route all incoming mail across multiple departments.

## üìå Problem  
Prior to implementing Stable, the organization faced several challenges:

- Mail was delivered physically to a central office  
- AP, Legal, Credentialing, Treasury, and Operations relied on in-person sorting  
- No visibility into routing logic or ownership  
- No audit trail for critical mail (legal notices, payer correspondence, provider documents)  
- Significant risk during an upcoming office move  
- Delays caused by misplaced or slow-routed mail  
- Manual scanning and distribution created bottlenecks  

These issues introduced financial, legal, and compliance risk ‚Äî especially during peak operational periods.

## üöÄ Solution ‚Äî Z$ Stable Mailroom Implementation  
Designed a full implementation plan from kickoff through go-live:

### **1. Project Plan & Timeline Architecture**  
- Built a comprehensive project plan aligned to a February 1 go-live  
- Created a phase-based timeline: discovery ‚Üí routing design ‚Üí SOP drafting ‚Üí USPS setup ‚Üí testing ‚Üí launch  
- Identified critical paths across all departments  

### **2. Routing Logic Design**  
- Mapped high-frequency mail categories  
- Designed rules for AP, Treasury, Legal, Credentialing, Provider Relations, and Operations  
- Established escalation triggers and fallback owners  
- Implemented logic for ‚Äúunknown‚Äù classification handling  

### **3. Department-Level SOPs**  
Created SOPs for:
- Mail intake & classification review  
- Routing logic updates  
- AP invoice + check workflows  
- Legal notices & compliance pathways  
- Provider credentialing document handling  
- Exception management & audit trail requirements  

Each SOP included:
- Ownership  
- Tools required  
- Step-by-step guidance  
- SLAs  
- Evidence collection rules  

### **4. USPS Form 1583 & Address Transition Management**  
- Guided the org through completion of Form 1583  
- Coordinated address changes across vendors and partners  
- Ensured USPS forwarding timelines aligned with Stable onboarding  
- Reduced mail disruption during the office move  

### **5. Testing & Validation Framework**  
- Built structured testing scenarios for multiple mail types  
- Designed validation logs  
- Ensured routing accuracy targets prior to go-live  

### **6. Go-Live Readiness Checklist**  
- Routing activated  
- All teams trained  
- Escalation contacts assigned  
- SOPs published in shared drive  
- Audit log processes turned on
