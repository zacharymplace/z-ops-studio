# Engagement Options  
### Clear structures for partnering with Z$ Ops Studio.

Z$ Ops Studio offers a flexible set of engagement models designed to match different types of operational needs — from quick-turn automations to full system redesigns.

The goal is always the same: bring clarity, control, and efficiency to your workflows.

---

## 1. Project-Based Work  
Structured, end-to-end builds for systems, automations, or documentation.

**Examples:**  
- Cash forecasting rebuilds  
- Ramp governance + access frameworks  
- Adaptive reporting redesign  
- SOP architecture & documentation systems  
- Digital mailroom implementation planning  

**Best for:**  
Teams needing clear deliverables and substantial operational upgrades.

---

## 2. Systems Packages  
Predefined systems delivered as complete operational kits.

**Examples:**  
- Finance Ops Kit (forecasting, reconciliations, dashboards)  
- LifeOS Systems (Notion/Airtable personal workflow frameworks)  
- Creative Ops Kits (music organization, event systems)  

**Best for:**  
Teams or individuals wanting a ready-made operational foundation.

---

## 3. Automation Retainers  
Ongoing access to automation engineering + quick-turn workflow enhancements.

**Includes:**  
- Monthly automation updates  
- Bug fixes  
- Small workflow builds  
- Monitoring & improvements  
- Documentation updates  

**Best for:**  
Teams with recurring processes that need continuous refinement.

---

## 4. Consulting & Advisory  
Expert guidance for system design, integrations, and operational strategy.

**Examples:**  
- Workday Adaptive → D365 mapping  
- COA alignment  
- Month-end close design  
- Treasury workflow structure  
- Systems implementation support (Ramp, Stable, etc.)  

**Best for:**  
Organizations needing strategic clarity and structured planning.
